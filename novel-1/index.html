The Long Game

Leo Van den Berg was a master of order—until a heart attack at his standing desk ended his quiet, predictable life with his final thought being a missed backup protocol. Death was supposed to be quiet. Instead, he wakes in a world of two moons, floating a thousand feet above a primeval forest with no body, no heartbeat, and a single notification: The Dungeon Creator System has been installed successfully. Reborn as a spectral Dungeon Master, Leo must build a dungeon from nothing—a job demanding the patience, precision, and ruthless optimization he spent a lifetime mastering. But in a realm where rivals play by chaos and gods watch with amusement, his methodical nature becomes both his greatest weapon and his deepest isolation. The Long Game is a dark fantasy gamelit novel about second chances, the beauty of systems, and a quiet man who discovers that death is just the beginning of his most important project.

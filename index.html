ArabicFix
active

Fixes Arabic text rendering for cross-platform servers — reshapes and reverses messages so Arabic reads correctly between Bedrock and Java players, in chat and on signs. Supports LuckPerms prefixes and DiscordSRV chat relay.

version:1.1 Server-version:1.21 - 26.2 Dependencies:None Support:LuckPerms, DiscordSRV, Floodgate, Geyser Server-Software:Spigot/Paper
DeathStorage
active

DeathStorage is a unique Minecraft plugin that revolutionizes the death experience! Instead of losing your items or having them scattered on the ground, this plugin creates or drops a special death chest that safely preserves all your inventory items in an organized chest GUI..

version:1.0.1 Server-version:1.21 - 26.2 Dependencies:None Server-Software:Spigot/Paper
